Useful words for technical writing

If you need more explantion or examples for these words, I encourage you to search in Cambridge Online Dictionary or Oxford Online Dictionary

    A

  • Affect - having an influence
  • Albeit - though
  • Ameliorate - to make something better
  • Anomaly - different from normal
  • Attribute - A quality or characteristic of something

    • C

  • Concomitant - Both the things happening are connected with one another
  • Commensurate - In proportion to another one

    • D

  • Despite - without being influenced

    • E

  • Effect - result of an influence
  • Eschew - to avoid something intentionally
  • et al. - and others

    • L

  • Leverage - to use something currently available things to achieve better

    • O

  • Owing to - because of

    • P

  • Perception - belief

    • S

  • Serendipity - discovering something intersting by chance
  • Subsume - to include something as a part of a larger group
