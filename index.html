Gollapalli Prince

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Metallurgical and Materials Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IITM), working in Dr. Satyesh Kumar Yadav's lab.

Before coming to IITM, I completed my Masters' degree in Materials Engineering at IISc Bangalore and Bachelors' degree in Metallurgical and Materials Engineering at NIT Warangal.

Research

I computationally screen combinations of metals and ceramics which favors atomic-level chemical gradation, using density functional theory (DFT). Such gradation at the interface helps to mitigate abrupt the change in materials' behavior at the interface. Metal/ceramic interface is encountered in several applications including wear-resistant coatings, thermal barrier coatings, diffusion barrier layers in electronic devices, energy materials, etc. I introduce vacancies and interstitials in the system and study energetics to gain insights at the atomic level.
