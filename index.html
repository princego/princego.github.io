Research Interests

About

Prince Gollapalli holds a B.Tech degree in Metallurgical and Materials Engineering from National Institute of Technology Warangal, and M.E in Materials Engineering from Indian Institute of Science Bangalore. He is currently pursuing Ph.D. in Metallurgical and Materials Engineering from Indian Institue of Technology Madras, and a visiting scholar in the School of Materials Engineering at Purdue University.

His work focuses on gaining atomic-level insights into anion migration from ceramic to metal by tapping into the predictive power of first-principles density functional theory based on the fundamental laws of quantum mechanics. He is also exploring into artificial intelligence to bridge the gap between physics-driven first-principles and data-driven machine learning approaches for better materials design.
