Research

I am trying to address the atomic level thermodynamic intermixing stability of metal/ceramic nano-heterostructures at the interface by computationally screening combinations of metals and ceramics, using first-principles density functional theory (DFT). Such interface is encountered in applications including wear-resistant coatings, diffusion barrier layers in electronic devices, energy materials, etc. I introduce vacancies and interstitials in the system and study their energetics to gain atomic level insights.

RESEARCH

To eplore the properties of new materials by bridging physics-driven first-principles and data-driven machine learning approaches.
